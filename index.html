      My Work

      A Study of Quasi-Normal Modes of Different Kinds of Black Holes

      Calculated Quasi-Normal Modes (QNMs) for Kerr Black Holes using a novel technique which I formulated, comparing results with Leaver's method, and investigated Kerr-Sen Black Holes to establish a relationship between shadow radius and quasi-normal frequencies. Currently working on QNMs for Kerr-Newman Black Holes and presented findings at a seminar hosted by the Physics Department at IIT Indore for my MSc thesis.

      Properties of Anti De Sitter Black Holes

      Studied AdS/CFT correspondence, black hole thermodynamics, holographic duality, string theory connection to QCD and the physics of D-branes. Calculated hydrodynimics and perturbations of anti de sitter black holes in d-dimension. Currently exploring finite-temperature field theory correlation functions and anti-de Sitter black holes, with all work presented in a seminar at IIT Indore.

      Unruh Radiation

      Explored General Relativity, Cosmology, and Black Holes, performing calculations in Quantum Field Theory (QFT) in curved spacetime. Presented findings at a seminar organized by the Physics Department, IIT Indore.
